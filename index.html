About

Quizzer. Quizmaster. Storyteller.

Competitive quizzing taught me how to answer great questions. Becoming a quizmaster taught me how to ask them.

The best quizzes don't just test what you know - they make you excited about what you don't. Every audience is different. Every quiz should be too.


That's why every quiz I create is built from scratch. No recycled question banks. No generic PowerPoint trivia. Just thoughtful, audience-first experiences designed to spark curiosity, conversation, and a healthy dose of competition.

Let's build something your attendees will still be talking about long after the final question.
